What We Treat

Conditions Treated

We provide evaluation and treatment for a broad range of psychiatric conditions across the lifespan.

🌑 Major Depressive Disorder

Persistent sadness, loss of interest, and changes in sleep, energy, and concentration. Depression is one of the most common and treatable mental health conditions.

😰 Generalized Anxiety Disorder

Chronic, excessive worry that is difficult to control and often accompanied by physical symptoms. Effective treatment combines medication and therapy.

🔄 Bipolar Disorder

A mood disorder characterized by episodes of depression and mania or hypomania. Careful medication management is central to stabilizing mood.

⚡ ADHD

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder affects focus, impulse control, and executive function. Comprehensive evaluation guides targeted treatment.

💔 PTSD

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder may develop following traumatic experiences. Trauma-informed care and evidence-based medication support recovery.

🧩 Psychotic Disorders

Including schizophrenia spectrum disorders characterized by hallucinations, delusions, or disorganized thinking. Ongoing psychiatric management is essential.

🌙 Insomnia & Sleep Disorders

Persistent difficulty initiating or maintaining sleep that impacts daily functioning. Treatment addresses both sleep and co-occurring psychiatric conditions.

🌊 Adjustment Disorders

Emotional or behavioral reactions to life stressors beyond what would typically be expected. Support-focused treatment helps patients regain stability.

🧓🏽 Geriatric Mood & Cognitive Concerns

Age-related changes in mood and cognition require specialized evaluation and careful prescribing. We provide expert care for older adults navigating these challenges.

Your First Appointment

Your initial appointment will be a comprehensive psychiatric evaluation. This visit is typically 60–90 minutes and includes:

Policies & Notices

Practice Policies

🔐 Notice of Privacy Practices (HIPAA)

Imani Behavioral Health Services, Inc. is committed to protecting your health information. As required by the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), we maintain the privacy of your protected health information (PHI).

Your PHI may be used for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations. We will not share your information beyond these purposes without your written authorization, except as permitted or required by law. You have the right to request access to your records, request corrections, and obtain an accounting of disclosures.

For questions regarding your privacy rights or to file a complaint, contact our office directly.

💻 Telehealth Consent

By scheduling and participating in telehealth appointments, patients acknowledge and consent to the following:

  • Telehealth services are provided via HIPAA-compliant, encrypted video platforms.
  • Telehealth has limitations and may not be appropriate for all clinical situations.
  • Technical issues may occasionally interrupt sessions; alternative arrangements will be made as needed.
  • Patients must be located within Massachusetts during telehealth sessions.
  • Telehealth is not appropriate for psychiatric emergencies. In a crisis, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.

💊 Controlled Substance Policy

The prescribing of controlled substances is subject to strict clinical guidelines in accordance with Massachusetts law and professional standards of practice. The following expectations apply:

  • A comprehensive psychiatric evaluation must occur before any controlled substance is prescribed.
  • A treatment agreement may be required prior to initiation of controlled substance therapy.
  • Prescription monitoring program (PDMP) checks are conducted as required.
  • Controlled substances will not be prescribed for lost, stolen, or destroyed prescriptions.
  • Early refill requests are generally not accommodated.
  • Failure to comply with treatment agreements may result in discontinuation of controlled substance prescribing.

♿ Accessibility Statement

Imani Behavioral Health Services, Inc. is committed to ensuring that our services are accessible to all individuals. If you require accommodations due to a disability, please notify our office at the time of scheduling so that we may make appropriate arrangements.

Our telehealth services provide increased accessibility for patients with mobility limitations or transportation challenges. For additional accessibility support, please contact our office.
